  - triangle (180) F#

F# Major Chord can be expressed by 3 dimensional shapes:
  
  - tetrahedron (4 * 180 = 720) F#
  - cube (6 * 360 = 2160) C#
  - octahedron (8 * 180 = 1440) F#
  - icosahedron (20 * 180 = 3600) A#
